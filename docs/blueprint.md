# **App Name**: OrngFinance

## Core Features:

- Global Asset Ticker: A high-performance real-time scrolling marquee featuring live prices for top-tier crypto assets and major forex currency pairs.
- Strategy Scout AI: An interactive tool that leverages generative AI to help users define their trading persona and suggests portfolio allocations based on current market sentiment.
- Modular Market Catalog: Minimalist card-based layouts for individual assets providing clear entry points for deep-dive technical analysis and trading views.
- Dynamic Reward Visualizer: An interactive slider interface that calculates potential staking rewards or interest yields for specific crypto-holdings.
- Waitlist Conversion Engine: A sleek, single-input capture form designed for maximum conversion and frictionless early-access signups.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant, high-energy sunset orange (#FF6600) to represent market volatility and opportunity.
- Background color: A deep, midnight obsidian (#140F0C) which provides a high-contrast foundation for the primary orange, reflecting a premium dark-mode aesthetic.
- Accent color: A soft, salmon-pink coral (#F2596D) used sparingly for notifications or highlights to provide an analogous warm contrast.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for its computerized, scientific precision. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for high legibility and a neutral, machined look.
- Thin-weight, 2px stroke linear icons. Icons should be monochrome with orange accents upon hover states to maintain a minimalist 'less is more' approach.
- Generous use of whitespace (negative space) and a modular bento-box grid system that mimics the structural efficiency of professional trading dashboards.
- Smooth, rhythmic fades for market price updates and staggered CSS-based entry transitions for page sections to emphasize fluid liquidity.
